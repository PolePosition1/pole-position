# Inspiration / Mood Board (LOCAL ONLY)

> **Do not push this folder to the public repo.** It's in `.gitignore`. Most of
> these carry real driver likeness and team IP (Ferrari, Mercedes, Red Bull).
> They're here as **creative reference for layout, type, and treatment** — the
> angle to borrow, never the IP to reproduce. This keeps the standing rule:
> *original Pole Position-native designs only, no driver likeness or team IP.*

Use these by pointing Claude Design at a description ("build a prediction card
in the spirit of the P1/P2/P3 layout, but Pole Position gold + checkered, no real
drivers"). The brand system in `/brand` is what governs the output.

## How these map to the brand
- The **gold + checkered + red** language in `01`, `02`, `05`, `08` is exactly the
  Champion palette the tokens now encode — these confirm the direction.
- `09` (start lights) and `05`/`07` (3D metallic & neon stamps) feed the signature
  motifs: five-lights row, gold-chrome "P1", "CALLED IT" stamp.
- Anything with a real driver/team is **reference only** — recreate the *treatment*
  with original, IP-clean Pole Position assets.
