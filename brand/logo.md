# Logo & Mark

The primary mark exists: **`assets/logos/pole-position-logo.png`** — a stylized
**"P"** built from a **black-and-white checkered flag**, edged in **metallic
champion gold**, with **red speed streaks** trailing the leading edge. It is the
source of the whole Champion palette.

## What the logo encodes (and the brand inherits)
- **Gold = pole / champion / winning.** The metallic edge is the signature color.
- **Checkered flag = the finish and the grid.** The brand's signature texture.
- **Red speed streaks = motion and urgency.** The accent.
- Works on **white or black**; richest on Lights Out `#0B0D12`.

## Lockups
- **Icon mark:** the gold checkered "P" alone (app icon, avatar, favicon, watermark).
- **Horizontal lockup:** the "P" mark + "POLE POSITION" set in **Saira 800**, all caps, tracked tight, white or gold, baseline-aligned to the mark.
- **Stacked lockup:** mark above "POLE POSITION" for square/profile placements.

> a horizontal + stacked wordmark lockup in Saira 800." Drop results in `assets/logos/`.

## Color treatments (priority order)
1. **Full logo** (gold + checkered + red) on Lights Out — default.
2. **Gold sheen** mark (`grad-gold`) on Lights Out — premium / hero / merch.
3. **Mono white** mark on Lights Out — small sizes, single-color contexts.
4. **Mono black** mark on white — light-background placements.

## Clear space & sizing
- Clear space on all sides = the height of the bowl of the "P".
- Minimum sizes: wordmark **24px** tall on screen; icon mark legible at **48×48px** down to a **16px** favicon (if the checker muddies at 16px, ship a simplified solid-gold "P" favicon).

## Misuse — never
- Don't recolor outside the treatments above (no purple, no teal).
- Don't add extra drop shadows, bevels, or outlines beyond the logo's own.
- Don't stretch, condense, rotate, or re-angle the speed streaks.
- Don't place on a busy photo without a Lights Out scrim behind it.
- Don't pair with driver/team logos or imagery.
